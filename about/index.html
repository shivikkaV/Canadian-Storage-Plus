Our Story

Storage Built for Eastern Ontario

Canadian Storage Plus was founded right here in Brockville with a simple idea: make storage accessible, affordable, and stress-free for everyone in Eastern Ontario. From the beginning, we built our facility with the customer experience in mind, not just the bottom line.

We serve a wide range of customers, from families going through home transitions and students heading off to school, to contractors needing on-site container solutions and boaters looking for a safe winter home for their watercraft. Whatever your situation, we have a solution that fits.

We are proud of our 4.8-star Google rating because it reflects real feedback from real neighbors. Our team is responsive, honest about pricing, and committed to making every customer feel like their belongings matter, because they do.
