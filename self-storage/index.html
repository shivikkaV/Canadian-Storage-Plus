About Our Units

Secure Storage That Works Around Your Life

Our self-storage units are built for everyday Canadians who need a reliable, no-fuss place to store their belongings. Whether you are decluttering, moving, renovating, or just running out of space, we have a unit that fits your situation and your timeline.

Every unit is individually alarmed and accessed through a gated keypad system that is available to tenants around the clock. Our facility is fully fenced with perimeter surveillance so your belongings are protected whether you visit daily or not for months.

We offer five unit sizes to cover everything from a student's seasonal items to the full contents of a family home. Month-to-month leasing means no long-term commitment, and our team is always available to help you pick the right size before you sign anything.

  • 24/7 electronic keypad gate access
  • Individual electronic unit alarms
  • CCTV surveillance throughout the facility
  • Fully fenced and lit perimeter
  • Drive-up units available for easy loading
  • Month-to-month leasing, no long-term lock-in
