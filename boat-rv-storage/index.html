About Our Lots

Keep Your Investment Safe All Season Long

Eastern Ontario is a paradise for boaters and outdoor enthusiasts, but the off-season can be tough on recreational vehicles left sitting in a driveway or side yard. Our boat and RV storage lots are designed to give your investment a proper home between adventures.

The facility is fully fenced, gated with keypad access, and monitored by surveillance cameras around the clock. Wide drive lanes make it easy to pull in and out even with a long trailer in tow, and our three lot sizes are designed to accommodate everything from a small fishing boat on a trailer to a full-sized Class A motorhome.

We welcome boats of all types, personal watercraft, travel trailers, fifth wheels, motorhomes, and utility trailers. Seasonal plans are available for customers who only need winter storage, as well as annual plans for those who prefer a dedicated spot year-round.

  • Fully fenced and gated perimeter with keypad access
  • CCTV surveillance throughout the facility
  • Wide drive lanes for easy maneuverability
  • Seasonal and annual plans available
  • Boats, RVs, trailers, and personal watercraft all welcome
  • Located minutes from Highway 401 in Brockville
